History

Whispering River Rafting was founded in 2005 by a group of adventure seekers who shared a deep love for rivers and the great outdoors. What started as a small operation with a single raft and big dreams has grown into one of the region’s most trusted whitewater rafting companies. Nestled near some of the most scenic and thrilling rivers in the country, we’ve spent nearly two decades guiding thousands of guests down rapids ranging from beginner-friendly to adrenaline-pumping.

Our mission has always been to provide safe, unforgettable adventures while respecting and preserving the natural beauty around us. With a team of certified guides, top-notch equipment, and a passion for adventure, Whispering River Rafting continues to deliver experiences that create memories for a lifetime. Whether you're a first-time paddler or a seasoned thrill-seeker, you’re part of our river family.

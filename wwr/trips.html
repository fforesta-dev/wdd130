Our Rafting Trips

Ready to book your adventure? Contact us today!

Scenic Canyon Float

Immerse yourself in nature on this peaceful float trip through a stunning canyon. Ideal for families and beginners, this 2-hour journey features calm waters, breathtaking cliffs, and occasional wildlife sightings such as deer, eagles, and herons. Our experienced guides ensure a safe and educational trip suitable for all ages.

Rapid Rush Adventure

Thrill-seekers will love this half-day experience packed with excitement. Navigate Class III and IV rapids with our certified guides while surrounded by rugged landscapes. Learn rafting techniques, teamwork, and enjoy adrenaline-pumping drops and splashes. Perfect for those looking for a bold challenge.

Ultimate Wilderness Expedition

This 3-day journey takes you deep into the heart of the wilderness. Raft through challenging rapids by day and relax at camp by night. Enjoy hearty meals, campfires under the stars, and the serenity of being off-grid. Designed for experienced adventurers ready to push their limits and create unforgettable memories.
